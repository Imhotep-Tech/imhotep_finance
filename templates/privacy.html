Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: January 30, 2025

Overview

At Imhotep Financial Manager, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Our Commitment:

We will never sell your personal data to third parties. Your financial information is encrypted and stored securely, and we only use it to provide you with our services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

Account Information

  • • Email address
  • • Username
  • • Password (encrypted)
  • • Profile preferences

Financial Data

  • • Income and expense records
  • • Account balances
  • • Transaction descriptions
  • • Financial goals and targets

Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use our service:

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Log files and error reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Service Provision

  • Provide and maintain our financial management tools
  • Calculate net worth and financial metrics
  • Generate reports and insights
  • Process transactions and updates

Account Management

  • Authenticate and verify your identity
  • Communicate important account updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Backup and restore your data

Service Improvement:

We may use aggregated, anonymized data to improve our services, but we never share individual user data without explicit consent.

3. Data Protection & Security

Security Measures

Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers with:

  • Regular automated backups
  • Redundant storage systems
  • Physical security controls
  • Network firewalls and monitoring

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Our Promise:

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our service (hosting, analytics, support)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with your consent)
  • Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may have access to limited data:

Google Analytics: For usage statistics (anonymized)

Google OAuth: For authentication (if you choose to sign in with Google)

Cloud Hosting: For secure data storage and application hosting

5. Your Rights & Controls

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Data Access

  • • View all your stored data
  • • Export your financial records
  • • Download account information
  • • Request data usage reports

Data Control

  • • Update or correct your information
  • • Delete specific records or entire account
  • • Opt out of communications
  • • Restrict data processing

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

  • Use the controls in your account settings
  • Contact our support team via email
  • We will respond to your request within 30 days
  • Some actions may require identity verification

6. Cookies & Tracking

What We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for basic functionality, authentication, and security. These cannot be disabled.

Preference Cookies

Store your settings like dark mode, currency preferences, and language choices.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how you use our service to improve functionality (anonymized data only).

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Your browser settings
  • Our cookie preferences (coming soon)
  • Opting out of analytics tracking

7. Data Retention

We retain your data according to the following schedule:

Active Account Data: As long as your account is active
Inactive Accounts: 2 years after last login
Deleted Accounts: 30 days (for recovery), then permanently deleted
Legal Requirements: As required by applicable law

Note:

Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained longer for service improvement purposes, but it cannot be linked back to your identity.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in various locations:

  • Our primary servers are located in secure data centers
  • Backup systems may be in different geographic regions
  • Some service providers may process data internationally
  • All transfers comply with applicable data protection laws

Protection Measures:

We ensure adequate protection for international transfers through appropriate safeguards, including encryption and contractual protections.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last updated" date at the top
  • We will notify you via email for significant changes
  • We may display an in-app notification
  • The updated policy will take effect 30 days after notification

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

Privacy Officer: privacy@imhotepfinance.com

General Support: support@imhotepfinance.com

Website: imhotepfinance.com

Response Time: We respond to privacy inquiries within 48 hours

Data Protection Rights:

You have the right to file a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe we have not addressed your privacy concerns adequately.
